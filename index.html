About Gann Square of 9

The Gann Square of Nine is a technical analysis tool developed by W.D. Gann, a famous financial trader. This mathematical instrument is based on the principle that price movements in financial markets are geometric and cyclical in nature.

The calculator derives potential support and resistance levels by calculating mathematical relationships between numbers in a spiral arrangement. These levels often correspond to key psychological points in the market and can help traders identify potential reversal areas.

While originally developed for stocks, the Gann Square of Nine can be applied to various markets including forex, commodities, and indices.

How To Use This Calculator
